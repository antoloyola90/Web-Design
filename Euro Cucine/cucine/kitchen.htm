Kitchen
Our kitchen cabinets are great show pieces of art in addition to being a high caliber utility item of any modern kitchen. Our kitchen cabinets are designed with multiple racks to store utensils that are regularly needed in the kitchen. There are different types of accessories to fit in the kitchen cabinets like pullout shelves, deep drawers for bulky vessels and pan storage with perfectly engineered functions. The cabinets are made with Water proof ply and prelaminated particle boards with both side lamination and shutters are made with medium density fibre. Membrane finish and hardwood doors also.

Accessories
The accessories we use are internationally trusted brands. We use stainless steel accessories from Kaff, Italy and soft close draw sliders Mepla, Germany. The Hardware can also be from Blum or Haffle’ as per customer’s requirement.

"Int-Hab" - "Intuitive Habitat"

Int-Hab a word play of the term "Intuitive Habitat", started by Ar. Sachin Shetty, A graduate from The Malnad School Of Architecture, Hassan, Karnataka. He pursued his masters in diploma in Construction Management from NICMAR, Pune.

Ar. Sachin Shetty started his professional career under the experience of Ar. Sashi Prabhu , Mumbai followed by a stint of a year at Jaspreet & Sagar and Dev Bildikar Associates , Bangalore.

A decade of his professional excellence was spent as one of the founders and principal architect of cube architects, Bangalore.
